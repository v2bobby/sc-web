Our Mission

SabajCore is the technological core of Nigerian real estate.

We reduce title verification from 3–6 months to seconds, eliminate fraudulent multiple-sales, and empower developers with real-time GIS intelligence — building the trust infrastructure Nigeria's property sector desperately needs.

By digitizing administrative bottlenecks and introducing transparency through decentralized ledger technology, SabajCore solves the trust deficit that has plagued land ownership and urban planning in Nigeria for decades.

We don't just build software. We build the foundational layer that every developer, buyer, government agency and investor will rely on.
