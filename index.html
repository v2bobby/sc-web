VISION OVERVIEW

SabajCore is positioned to be the technological “core” of the Nigerian real estate and property development sector.

By digitizing administrative bottlenecks and introducing transparency through decentralized ledger technology, SabajCore solves the trust deficit in land ownership and urban planning that has plagued the sector for decades.

We eliminate fraudulent multiple-sales, reduce title verification from 3–6 months to seconds, and give developers real-time, data-driven insights into where to build next.

PRODUCT SUITE

Three pillars. One unbreakable core.

The Ledger

Smart-Title Verification

A blockchain-based verification system that allows users to instantly check the status, history, and authenticity of land titles. This reduces the traditional 3-6 month verification window to mere seconds, protecting buyers from fraudulent multiple-sales once and for all.
